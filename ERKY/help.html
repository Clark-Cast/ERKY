Follow the steps below to navigate ERKY STORE.
  1. Register - if you don't have an account;
    Login - if you already have an account.


  2. Browse items - find the items that you really like to buy.


  3. Add to Cart - if you really like the product, just simply click the cart icon.


  4. Go to Cart - this is where your items goes after that. You can also update or remove them if you change your mind.


  5. Checkout - Want the item? Check it out.

  6. Fill me up - Just give the required information and then you have it.

  7. Wait for it! - Estimated 1-3 days delivery.
